Web Developmet with NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day
The APOD (Astronomy Picture of the Day) is a NASA service that offers daily image or snapshot of our universe along with a brief explanation given by an expert astonomer. Everyday scine it began on June 16, 1995, a new image has appeared on it. While APOD strives to post a new image every day, there have occasionally been reprints or cases in which an image from a previous date may have been used. Additionally, the picture for th current day might not yet be accescible depending on when you request the API.
